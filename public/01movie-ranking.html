The Best Movies according to Kálmán

My top 5 movies of all time

1. - Man From Earth

Super intelligent movie about a bunch of university professors exploring the possibility of a crazy idea.

2. - Midnight Run

Super funny and surprisingly warm buddy comedy about a bounty hunter (De Niro) taking a mob accountant back to the bail bond agency, where he will surely meet his early demise - but the trip takes much longer than expected.

3. - Jesus Christ Superstar

Beautifully made musical with the best rock opera songs of all time.

4. - Nimona

Incredibly funny, and beautifully made animated move about a shapeshifter befriending a knight in a knightpunk scifi story about discrimination and standing up for what's right.

4. - I'm a Cyborg, but that's OK

Crazy Korean movie about a bunch of seriously scizophrenic people living in an asylum, who face a brand new challenge when the new joiner looks like she'll die of starvation because she cannot eat human food - as she's convinced she's a cyborg.
